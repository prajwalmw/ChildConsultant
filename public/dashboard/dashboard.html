WHO Growth Standards Certified

All growth percentile calculations follow the official World Health Organization (WHO) Child Growth Standards (2006). These measurements use the internationally recognized LMS (Lambda-Mu-Sigma) method for accurate percentile calculations, ensuring compliance with global health standards used by healthcare professionals worldwide.
